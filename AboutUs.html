CSquare opened its doors in 2001 as Gurugram first European style bistro. It’s too early to say we’ve made history, but it has become what you might refer to as an institution.
The idea was to start create something sophisticated, but fun and casual in equal amounts. So that’s what we did, and that’s what we intend to keep doing. The team on board consist of professionals from the food industry to assure the best quality food.

We have a group of talented Directors:

  • THE CSquare PHILOSOPHY

We’re dead serious about our cooking, but like to serve it in an atmosphere that’s friendly and laidback. Good food can make for great social experiences, and we understand that better than most. You’ll see what we mean.

  • COMITTED TO CONFORT

Don’t hesitate to ask for whatever you need, and don’t be surprised to find yourselves on a first name basis with our servers. We love getting to know people. Your preferences and expectations might differ, but that’s no reason not to meet them.
